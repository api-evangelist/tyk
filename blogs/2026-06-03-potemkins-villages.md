Tyk CEO James Hirst critiques competitors using fake accounts to artificially build consensus on Reddit and Hacker News for AI search optimization, comparing it to historical Potemkin villages. He argues this strategy will fail because AI models increasingly value genuine reputation signals, and advocates instead for earning visibility through authenticity and helpful contributions.
